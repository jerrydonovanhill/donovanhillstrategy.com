About Jerry Varnado

Founder of Donovan Hill Strategy, Jerry brings over 15 years of experience as a Chief of Staff and strategy executive at some of the world's most innovative companies.

His unique background includes leadership roles at Meta, where he helped scale operations during hypergrowth periods, Stripe, where he worked on international expansion strategy, and ChargePoint, where he led strategic initiatives during their transition to public markets.

Jerry founded Donovan Hill Strategy to bring this operational expertise to founders and investors who need strategic thinking combined with hands-on execution.
